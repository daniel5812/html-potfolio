My top 3 movies of all-time.

1."The Godfather" (1972)

Directed by Francis Ford Coppola, this crime drama is based on the novel of the same name by Mario Puzo. It stars Marlon Brando, Al Pacino, and James Caan, and is renowned for its storytelling, acting, and memorable quotes.

2."Citizen Kane" (1941)

Directed by Orson Welles, this film is often regarded as a landmark in cinema history. It's a mystery-drama that follows the life of a wealthy newspaper magnate and explores themes of power and the human condition.

3."The Shawshank Redemption" (1994)

Directed by Frank Darabont, this drama is based on a Stephen King novella. It tells the story of a man's experiences in prison, emphasizing friendship, hope, and resilience. The film has gained a strong following since its release.
